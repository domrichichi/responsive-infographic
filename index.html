Section 3 Title: What’s Holding Back Connected Gyms

The variety of exercises that can be performed in the gym mean that there is no one-size-fits-all sensor for tracking everything. Rather, there needs to be a suite of sensors for different equipment and exercises, and a way to connect all the systems
